5.1. Mailing list

The debian-bsd mailing list is the best way to contact fellow users and developers for general help, development questions or any other related queries.

5.2. Wiki

The Debian GNU/kFreeBSD wiki contains lots of useful information, including an FAQ.

5.3. IRC

Various users and developers can be found in the #debian-kbsd IRC channel on the oftc network (irc.oftc.net). Keep in mind, most users in this channel are in different timezones all around the world, so if nobody happens to be around, try again at a different time of day.
