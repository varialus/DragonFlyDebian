Ging, the only live distribution based on Debian GNU/kFreeBSD, has made its way to the 0.1.0 release. Ging is now using KDE 3.4 as its desktop. Includes a very complete set of desktop applications: Konqueror, Koffice, Gimp, Gaim, and even a few SDL-based games featuring penguins! (who'd be afraid of such a nice animal..). For those around who have tried previous versions of Ging, you'll be greatly pleased to see the boot time has decreased by a factor of ~15 times.
